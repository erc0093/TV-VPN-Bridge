# TV VPN Bridge

Share your Mac VPN connection to LG TV and other devices, without routers or extra setup.

## What It Does

TV VPN Bridge lets you use your Mac as a simple network bridge for TVs and other devices.

It is designed for users who:
- use a VPN or proxy on Mac
- want to watch native streaming apps on LG TV or similar devices
- do not want to set up a router or soft router
- just want a simple desktop app

## Download

Go to the latest release and download the app package.

## Features

- Share your Mac VPN connection to TV and other devices
- Works with TUN-based VPN or proxy connections
- Simple start / stop workflow
- License activation support
- Built-in purchase and support group info

## Requirements

- macOS
- A working VPN / proxy connection on your Mac
- A wired connection setup that matches your device environment

## Notes

On first launch, macOS may show a security warning because the app is not yet signed and notarized.

If that happens:
1. Open the app once
2. Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security
3. Allow the app manually
4. Open it again
